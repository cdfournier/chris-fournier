Introduction to Enservio Customer Portal

User Experience DesignEnservio

A collaborative effort to update and enhance the user experience and create a design system for the Enservio Customer Portal.

Enservio project details

Deliverables

UX Design System, HTML, and SCSS/CSS

Brief

Design a user experience and develop a prototype to improve data capture and customer satisfaction.

Audience

Insurance carriers looking to provide their adjusters and insureds with an improved and intuitive solution to collect damage data when a catastrophe strikes.

Strategy

The primary strategy was: keep it simple. We didn’t want to unnecessarily overcomplicate things that should be simple. We committed ourselves to challenging all of the “what if” scenarios that can arise during agile planning sessions.

We also set ourselves up for incremental and iterative success by working together to write excellent agile stories. Working together as a team we were very prepared and flexible, and this enabled us to fine tune existing work, add new features, or change direction as needed. Working in bite sized chunks made every opportunity a possibility.

Intended Outcomes

Deliver a functional prototype that could be used to build consensus with internal and external stakeholders and that would also be viable enough to hand over to React engineers for development.

This was a truly wonderful project that really plugged into my passion for collaboration and detail-oriented project management. Weeks were spent acclimating to the existing product. After starting with a very small team of three, we continued to add more and more partners to the project in a methodical manner to support our agile approach.
