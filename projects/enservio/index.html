User Experience DesignEnservio

Enservio provides software and services for insurance companies.

Enservio Project Details

Deliverables

UX Design System, HTML, and SCSS/CSS

Brief

Design a user experience and develop a prototype to improve data capture and customer satisfaction.

Audience

Insurance carriers looking to provide their adjusters and insureds with an improved and intuitive solution to collect damage data when a catastrophe strikes.

Strategy

The primary strategy was: keep it simple. We committed ourselves to challenging all of the “what if” scenarios that can arise during agile planning sessions.

We also set ourselves up for incremental and iterative success by working together to write excellent agile stories. Collaborating as a team, we were prepared and flexible, enabling us to fine tune existing work, add new features, or change direction as needed. Working in bite sized chunks meant we were nimble and could tackle every opportunity.

Intended Outcomes

Deliver a functional prototype that could be used to build consensus with internal and external stakeholders and that would also be viable enough to hand over to React engineers for development.
