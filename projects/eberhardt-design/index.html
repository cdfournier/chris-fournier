Introduction to Eberhardt Design

Website DesignEberhardt Design

Eberhardt Design is a New York based product design agency.

Eberhardt Design Project Details

Deliverables

Strategy, web design, HTML, and SCSS/CSS

Brief

Refresh the site experience and update the content for the Eberhardt Design website.

Audience

Small business and startup founders, non-profit, and civic leaders looking to improve their product design.

Strategy

Our goal was to keep the experience and interface clean and simple, utilizing a one-page strategy for efficiency and to encourage client inquiries.

I worked with the team to hone the mission statement so that it would be both original and impactful. We wanted users to see this statement with a clear call-to-action as soon as the page loads.

Intended Outcomes

The goal was to attract potential clients looking to find a small, but experienced and strategic, design partner for their business. A secondary intent was to get the site into production as quickly as possible so that we could test the UX through outreach campaigns, iterating as needed.

After the initial discovery and launch of the first design phase, I plan to implement a second phase of UX improvements, including different font and color choices, larger font sizes, and graceful transitions and animation. The result is a bolder look that more confidently conveys the agency’s mission, skills, services, and experience.
