Introduction to TransferX

Case StudyTransferX

TransferX is a blockchain-driven P2P money transfer platform.

TransferX

Deliverables

Product design, logo design, branding, and UX design

Brief

Design a premium user experience and brand identity for blockchain financial services startup based in the UK.

Audience

Individuals who want a secure and easy way to transfer money to friends and family, especially in unbanked parts of the world.

Strategy

Design intuitively modern and sophisticated native app screens for a simple user experience that feels as familiar as fiat payment apps.

Intended Outcomes

Deliver a minimal viable product to test with early customers in eastern African nations like Kenya, Somalia and Tanzania; advise on future product development.

The Detail

Fintech apps focused on helping facilitate P2P crypto and fiat transfers, especially in developing and largely unbanked regions, are one of the most viable uses for blockchain. To foster the adoption of crypto payments, the app UX must feel as seamless to the user as using popular conventional payment apps.

My Process

I conducted a series of interviews with people involved in the blockchain space to better understand the opportunities and challenges. Based on these insights, I envisioned a user experience that would focus on:

  • A modern, mobile-first responsive design
  • Simplified navigation native and non-native crypto users
  • Complex options for payments, including repeat and automated payment scheduling abilities.

Designing the app screens was fun but challenging. It was important, even for the MVP release of the app, to design and deliver screens for complete functionality.

Outcome

The end result is a beautiful, modern, and intuitive app that makes transferring funds easy and enjoyable.
