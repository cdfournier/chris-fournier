Case studyTransferX

New branding and improved user experience design for financial services blockchain startup.

Introduction

TransferX is a blockchain-driven P2P money transfer platform designed to provide a better solution for transferring funds into under-banked markets and give money back to those same communities that need it.

Project details

  • BriefDesign a premium brand identity and user experience for a blockchain financial services startup based in the UK; must feel as comfortable as conventional finance applications.
  • AudienceIndividuals in eastern African nations like Kenya, Ethiopia, and Tanzania who want a secure and easy way to transfer money to friends and family.

Brand design

The biggest challenge on this project was the confluence of brand design iteration and user experience design and development. To get started on the branding I created a mood board with images of people, places, and things.

Color palette

The color palette came to life quickly, inspired by the arts and traditions of eastern African communities. The founders loved the balance between the really bright brand colors and the almost institutional darker colors.

Patterns and textures

Next I designed some patterns — still taking inspiration from the people of Kenya, Ethiopia, and Tanzania — in order to provide some subtle texture. The founders stressed that they did not want the brand to feel “sterile”.

Logo design

When it came to the logo, I started by designing the icon. I wanted the icon to express confidence and stability. The X in TransferX became an arrow to signify transactions and progress. I wanted something that would look good in any form of branding, as well as stand out in a crowd of blockchain logos and icons.

User experience design

The project brief for the native application user experience design stressed the importance of three key priorities:

  1. A modern financial services design
  2. Simplified navigation for native and non-native crypto users
  3. Complex options for payments, including repeat and automated payment scheduling abilities

I designed beautiful, modern, and intuitive app that made transferring funds easy and enjoyable. Unfortunately, the project was unable to secure funding to keep it going to the next phase.
