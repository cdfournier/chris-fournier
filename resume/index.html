Chris Fournier's Resume

Hi. My name is Chris Fournier

I’m a seasoned user experience designer who works in code and Git.

I specialize in UX and UI design, responsive web design, and design systems, and I have experience solving problems in cross-functional settings for a wide range of B2B and B2C products, platforms, and services for retail, e-commerce, fintech, and more.

Experience

TigerConnect2023 — today

Lead Product Designer

  • Crafts, refines, implements, and communicates design vision and strategy across the organization and champion user-centered design principles and methodologies.
  • Designs highly intuitive and elegant experiences that project simplicity within a very complex system, always keeping user needs and functional requirements at the fore.
  • Partners with product leadership to visualize the future of TigerConnect products, and collaborates with product managers, engineers, and other cross functional teams to streamline the discovery to delivery process, ensuring what is designed is effectively and efficiently built.
