East Wind Project Details

Deliverables

Branding, logo and icon design, and merchandising assets

Brief

Create a logo and icon for an exclusive private property on the island of Antigua. The client requested that the design evoke an Asian influence, that purple be incorporated into the design, and that the logo could be easily utilized across all branding applications without modification.

Audience

High net-worth individuals looking for a luxury property to lease in Antigua.

Intended Outcomes

A logo that embodies an upscale, but laid-back and casual island feel that appeals to high net worth guests and also drives awareness and rentals for the property.

My Process

This project was on a tight timeline. After gathering background information about the property, as well as looking at other properties on the island for inspiration, I brainstormed and analyzed ideas, designed concepts, and collaborated with the client for feedback daily for the duration of the project.
