TransferX

Case StudyTransferX

Product design and branding

Brief

Design a premium user experience and brand identity for ambitious financial services startup company based in the UK.

Audience

Individuals (based primarily in the UK) who want a quick and easy way to transfer money to friends, family and others in unbanked parts of the world.

Strategy

Design intuitively simple, modern and polished native app screens and user experiences for extensive financial services user journeys.

Intended Outcomes

Deliver a minimal viable product to test with early customers in eastern African nations like Kenya, Somalia and Tanzania and provide feedback for future product development, as well as drive investor interest.

After designing a new logo for the company, the next challenge was to come up with a color palette that was both clean and modern, but would also reflect the vibrant colors of eastern African nations. I was pleased to pair color options with patterns that were both tribal and modern.

Designing the app screens was fun but challenging. It was important, even for the MVP release of the app, to design and deliver the screens required for complete functionality. This included the complex options for payments, including repeat and automated payment scheduling abilities.

The end result is a beautiful, modern, and intuitive app that makes transferring funds easy and enjoyable.
